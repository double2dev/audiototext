# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Our web platform is a continuously deployed SaaS application. Therefore, only the **current live version** of [FreeAudioToText](https://freeaudiototext.com) is actively maintained and supported.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Security and privacy are the core pillars of FreeAudioToText. 

If you discover a security vulnerability, we strongly encourage you to report it to us immediately. Please **do not** disclose the vulnerability publicly in an issue or on social media before we have had a chance to address it.

To report a vulnerability, please email our security team at:
**support@freeaudiototext.com**

Please include:
1. A detailed description of the vulnerability.
2. Steps to reproduce the issue.
3. The potential impact of the vulnerability.

We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours and strive to send you regular updates about our progress. Thank you for keeping our community safe.
